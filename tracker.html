Tips for Reducing Your Environmental Impact

Energy Use

  • -Use appliances with the EU Energy Label to ensure high energy efficiency.
  • -Install a smart thermostat to optimise heating and cooling schedules.
  • -Turn off lights and unplug devices when they are not in use.
  • -Consider installing solar panels or exploring other renewable energy options.

Transportation

  • -Share rides with others to reduce individual vehicle emissions.
  • -Use public transit instead of driving alone.
  • -Opt for cycling or walking for short trips to reduce carbon emissions.
  • -Maintain your vehicle to ensure optimal fuel efficiency.

Waste Production

  • -Separate recyclable materials from general waste.
  • -Compost organic waste to reduce landfill contributions.
  • -Avoid single-use plastics by opting for reusable items.
  • -Donate or sell items you no longer need to extend their lifecycle.
